Parque de las Américas — Site Plan

Site Name

Parque de las Américas Mérida

This name was selected because it clearly identifies one of Mérida’s most important cultural parks. The title helps visitors easily recognize the location while promoting tourism, community activities, and cultural events held in the park.

Optional domain: parqueamericasmerida.org

Site Purpose

The website will provide information about Parque de las Américas in Mérida, Yucatán, including park history, activities, cultural events, attractions, visitor information, and recommendations for things to do. The site will serve as a guide for tourists and local residents looking for recreation, culture, and community events.

Scenarios

  • What activities can I do at Parque de las Américas with my family?
  • What cultural events or performances happen at the park?
  • Where is the park located and what are the visiting hours?

Color Scheme

Beige (#F5E6D3)
Used for page background
Terracotta (#C96A3D)
Used for headings, buttons, and accents

The color palette reflects Mérida’s warm climate and colonial architecture while creating a welcoming and cultural atmosphere.

Typography

Heading Font: Playfair Display — used for titles and headings.

Body Font: Open Sans — used for paragraphs and general content.
